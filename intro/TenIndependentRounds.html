Low-income members are given ten independent chances to increase their income. This means that low-income members will play ten independent rounds.

Each new round is independent of what happened in previous rounds.
In each new round, the initial income of the low-income group is £{{ session.config.low_income }} and the initial income of the high-income group is £{{ session.config.high_income }}.
In each new round, each low-income member is endowed with {{ session.config.initial_action_points }} Action Points and must use these points in the three ways described before to increase his/her income.

At the end of each round, low-income members will receive feedback about income redistribution in this round and whether they moved to the high-income group in this round.

Each participant will receive as bonus payment the final income of his group in one randomly selected round.
