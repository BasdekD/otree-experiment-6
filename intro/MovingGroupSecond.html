Moving to the High-income Group {{ endblock }} {{ block content }} {{extends "_static/global/timer_display_setup.html"}}

Some of the twelve rounds are moving rounds. In a moving round, the two, out of the four, low-income members who allocated the most Action Points to their personal account will move to the high-income group.

According to participants of previous sessions, the probability that each round is a moving round is {{ if session.config.mobility == 'low' }} 10%{{ else }} 50%{{ endif }}.

If the hypothetical round below is a moving round, low-income members A and C will move to the high-income group and will receive the final income of the high-income group.

However, if this round is not a moving round, all low-income members will remain to the low-income group and will receive the final income of this group.
